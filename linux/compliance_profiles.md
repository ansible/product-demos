# Supported Compliance Profiles                                                                             
 
The following compliance profiles are supported by the [**Linux / Enforce Compliance**](README.md#jobs) job template:
 
| **Profile** | **RHEL 8 Role Repository** | **RHEL 9 Role Repository** | **RHEL 10 Role Repository** |
|-------------|---------------------|---------------------|---------------------|
| CIS | https://github.com/RedHatOfficial/ansible-role-rhel8-cis | https://github.com/RedHatOfficial/ansible-role-rhel9-cis | https://github.com/RedHatOfficial/ansible-role-rhel10-cis
| CJIS | https://github.com/RedHatOfficial/ansible-role-rhel8-cjis |
| CUI | https://github.com/RedHatOfficial/ansible-role-rhel8-cui | https://github.com/RedHatOfficial/ansible-role-rhel9-cui
| HIPAA | https://github.com/RedHatOfficial/ansible-role-rhel8-hipaa | https://github.com/RedHatOfficial/ansible-role-rhel9-hipaa| https://github.com/RedHatOfficial/ansible-role-rhel10-hipaa
| OSPP | https://github.com/RedHatOfficial/ansible-role-rhel8-ospp | https://github.com/RedHatOfficial/ansible-role-rhel9-ospp | https://github.com/RedHatOfficial/ansible-role-rhel10-ospp
| PCI-DSS | https://github.com/RedHatOfficial/ansible-role-rhel8-pci-dss | https://github.com/RedHatOfficial/ansible-role-rhel9-pci-dss| https://github.com/RedHatOfficial/ansible-role-rhel10-pci-dss
| DISA STIG | https://github.com/RedHatOfficial/ansible-role-rhel8-stig | https://github.com/RedHatOfficial/ansible-role-rhel9-stig | https://github.com/RedHatOfficial/ansible-role-rhel10-stig 
 
These roles are derived from the [Compliance as Code](https://github.com/ComplianceAsCode/content) project, which provides SCAP content used by the [OpenSCAP](https://www.open-scap.org/) `oscap` tool.
